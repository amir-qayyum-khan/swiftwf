Arithmetic Magic

Kid-Safe Math Learning & Privacy Summary

A comprehensive math learning app for children ages 4–9.

Our Promise

Arithmetic Magic is designed for young learners to master math concepts in a safe, offline environment. The app never calls external APIs, never connects to the internet, and never asks for personal details—no names, emails, logins, or analytics. Every progress record and preference stays on the child’s device and disappears when parents delete the app.

Four Learning Sections

1. Learn

Children choose from Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division, or Exponentiation. They solve problems tailored to their level with voice guidance.

2. Random

A mixed mode featuring random questions from all operations with a timer to challenge the learner. Difficulty adapts as the child progresses.

3. Manual

Visual guides explaining how to use the app, ensuring parents and kids can navigate easily.

4. Settings

Control difficulty levels, toggle success animations, enable/disable speech, and manage history.

Interactive Play & Speech

  • Interactive Problems: Kids see a math problem (e.g., 5 + 2) and choose from 4 options. Correct answers advance the level.
  • Voice Guidance: The app speaks the problem to the child, aiding understanding and engagement.
  • Help Feature: Stuck? A futuristic help button demonstrates how to solve the problem step-by-step.
  • Offline Play: Works perfectly without an internet connection on phones, tablets, and Chromebooks.

Privacy at the Core

  • No accounts or identifiers: We never request names, photos, emails, or birthdates.
  • No network calls: Arithmetic Magic does not connect to servers, third-party APIs, ads, or analytics.
  • On-device only: Progress and settings live in local storage. Clearing the app or uninstalling it erases everything.
  • Safe Environment: A black-themed, distraction-free interface designed for focus.

Family Controls

  1. Open the Settings tab to manage difficulty levels and animations.
  2. Toggle speech on or off according to the learning environment.
  3. Delete history to reset progress and start fresh.

Your child's data never leaves the device, giving you complete peace of mind.
