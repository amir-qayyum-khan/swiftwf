About PortfolioPulse

PortfolioPulse helps investors make informed decisions by analyzing diversified portfolios across global markets. The app converts complex market data, news, and macroeconomic signals into structured, actionable insights — delivered in a clean, mobile-friendly format.

What You Can Analyze

  • Stocks
  • ETFs
  • Mutual Funds
  • Crypto Assets
  • Gold & Commodities

Users simply enter their portfolio allocation (totaling 100%), select their country or market, and receive a comprehensive analytical report.

Data Protection & Privacy

Privacy Commitment for PortfolioPulse Users

  1. No Sensitive Data Collection: We do not collect personal identifiers such as names, emails, phone numbers, or exact financial account details. The app is designed to function without requiring any personal information.
  2. On-Device Storage: All portfolio allocations (e.g., 20% in Gold, 80% in Stocks) and data entered by the user are saved exclusively on the user's device. We do not sync your portfolio data to our servers or maintain any central database of user portfolios.
  3. Real-Time Data Usage: To provide up-to-date market news and sentiment tracking, the app takes the user's selected country or market as input from a provided dropdown. This information is used to fetch real-time news and macro-economic signals from the internet.
  4. Data Discard Policy: Information used for real-time analysis is processed in-memory to generate your report. This data is not saved or stored after usage; it is discarded immediately once the analysis is complete.

Key Capabilities

  • Portfolio performance and risk assessment
  • Macro-economic impact analysis (interest rates, inflation, currency)
  • Market news and sentiment tracking with source links
  • Asset-level insights and benchmarking
  • SIP and allocation guidance
  • Rule-based exit and rebalance signals

User Responsibility

Users should double-check all information entered, including portfolio allocations and market selections, to ensure the resulting analysis is accurate and relevant to their needs.

Designed for Serious Investors

PortfolioPulse focuses on long-term wealth building, capital preservation, and disciplined decision-making. Outputs are concise, objective, and optimized for mobile consumption — no noise, no hype.

Disclaimer
