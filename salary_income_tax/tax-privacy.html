Data Protection & Privacy

Privacy Policy for Pak Salaried Tax Advisor

  1. Data Collection and Usage: We value your privacy. We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers. The app collects financial inputs (such as annual salary, age, and loan details) solely to generate your custom financial plan.
  2. Data Transmission (The RAG API): To provide you with a customized financial plan, the financial data you enter is transmitted securely (via HTTPS) to our processing server. This data is processed in real-time by our analysis model to generate your report. We do not store, save, or share this data. Once the calculation is returned to your device, the data is immediately discarded from our server.
  3. Payments: This app offers paid features. Payments are processed securely through Google Play's billing system. We do not access, store, or collect your credit card or banking information directly. All transaction data is handled in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Your Data, Your Privacy

We do not collect, store, or transmit any sensitive personal information to our servers.

All financial data remains exclusively on your device unless you initiate a calculation, in which case it is discarded right after the result is returned.

Data Storage & Export

  • On-Device Storage Only: Calculations, plan entries, and documents are written to your device memory—nothing is synced to our servers.
  • PDF Export: Generate and download tax reports as PDF documents for your records.
  • Data Control: You have complete control over your plans and can remove them instantly.
  • No Account Required: Use the app without creating an account or providing personal information.

Deleting Your Data

  • In-App Delete Buttons: Every savings plan section shows both Save and Delete. Tap Delete to remove that specific entry immediately.
  • Clear App Storage: On Android, open Settings → Apps → Pak Salaried Tax Advisor → Storage & cache and tap Clear cache or Clear storage to wipe everything.

Because we never copy your data to external servers, deleting it from your device permanently erases it.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't collect your name, email, or personal identifiers.
  • We don't store your salary details or financial information.
  • We don't track your usage patterns or behavior.
  • We don't share data with advertisers or third-party services.
  • We don't create user profiles or maintain databases of user information.

Key Features

  • Tax Planning Wizard: Guides you through key areas for tax reduction based on your personal financial data.
  • Interactive Savings Calculator: Estimates your potential tax savings based on the new TY 2026 slabs.
  • Covers Key Sections: Provides guidance on Zakat (Sec 60), Voluntary Pension (Sec 63), Housing Loan Credit (Sec 63A), Charitable Donations (Sec 61), and more.
  • Salary Structure Analysis: Helps you understand the tax impact of various allowances versus company-provided benefits.
