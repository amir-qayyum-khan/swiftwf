ABC Magic

Kid-Safe Learning & Privacy Summary

A handwriting-friendly game for children ages 4–7.

Our Promise

ABC Magic is built for young learners to explore letters safely. The app never calls external APIs, never connects to the internet, and never asks for personal details—no names, emails, logins, or analytics. Every letter trace, word attempt, and preference stays on the child’s device and disappears when parents delete the app.

Four Simple Tabs

1. Learn

Children see one delightful word at a time, hear it spoken slowly, and practice tracing with our custom keyboard.

2. Exercise

Kids can pick themed groups—nature, fruits, ocean creatures, and more. The interface mirrors Learn but only shows the selected category, reinforcing spelling through repetition.

3. Manual

Step-by-step visuals explain how each tab works so guardians can guide play or let kids explore independently.

4. Settings

Parents reset progress, hide or reveal helper words on Learn/Exercise, and control whether the narrator repeats words.

Words & Speech

  • Word lists are handpicked from child-safe Google vocabulary references during development and bundled with the app.
  • No live downloads occur; everything is stored locally for offline play.
  • The narrator pronounces each word slowly, giving little learners time to hear, type, and remember.

Privacy at the Core

  • No accounts or identifiers: We never request names, photos, emails, or birthdates.
  • No network calls: ABC Magic does not connect to servers, third-party APIs, ads, or analytics.
  • On-device only: Progress stars and settings live in local storage. Clearing the app or uninstalling it erases everything.
  • Kid-friendly keyboard: The custom keyboard prevents accidental data entry outside the app.

Family Controls

  1. Open the Settings tab to reset all progress or delete saved words instantly.
  2. Toggle “Show Helper Word” to hide on-screen hints on Learn and Exercise screens.
  3. Switch auto narration on/off so the app continues (or stops) speaking new words.

Because no information leaves the device, these controls offer complete household privacy.
